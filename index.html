About The MUN

Model United Nations is an authentic simulation of the UN General Asembly, UN Security Council, or other multilateral bodies, which catapults participants into the world of diplomacy and negotiation. In Model UN, participants step into the shoes of ambassadors of UN member states, from Afghanistan to Zimbabwe to debate current issues on the Organization's vast agenda.

 
Three Committees will be simulated this year

The United Nations Security Council is one of the six principal organs of the United Nations, charged with ensuring international peace and security, acepting new members to the United Nations and approving any changes to its charter

The United Nations General Assembly is one of the six principal organs of the United Nations, the only one in which all the member nations have equal representation , and the main deliberative, policy-making, and representative organ of the UN.

The Union Council of Ministers exercises executive authority in the Republic of India. It consists of senior ministers, called cabinet ministers, junior ministers, called ministers of state and, rarely, deputy ministers. It is led by the Prime Minister.

IISER Bhopal is an autonomous research and education institute. It was established by the MHRD, Government of India in 2008 in order to incorporate research in basic science at undergraduate and graduate level, with equal emphasis on higher education for research and education in science. The institute is committed to develop strong scientific thinking, impart high moral and ethical values to students and create a genuine concern for social and environmental issues.
